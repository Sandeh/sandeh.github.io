Losing Your Sh*t

A game made with Adam Sims as part of a game jam. "Retail work sucks right? You slave away all day for minimum wage, working for customers who frankly don't give a sh*t about you. In this game you can let out the anger of working with customers by yeeting their lost goods straight back at them! WITH NO CONSEQUENCES! This game tracks the time it takes to complete levels, so share with your friends and see who is quicker!"
